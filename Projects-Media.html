Projects/Media

Axis Reloaded

Worked with Eric Wells and Mark Sherstan to design, construct, and program a robotic kinectic sculpture as a hardware demo for the University of Alberta Faculty of Engineering.

The "claw" uses a machine vision (webcam in the palm) to track deviations in the rotating plate, to trigger a re-center. The whole device is self-contained and autonomous, using a raspberry pi for trajectory calculation and device control. Also demo'd at the January 2020 edition of Telus World of Science's Dark Matters.
